Places to Visit

Amazing Places to Visit

St. Mary's Island

St. Mary's Island is endowed with incredible natural splendor, and it is actually a group of four small islands in the Arabian Sea, just off the shore of Malpe Beach. Malpe's St. Mary's Island has an intriguing history. Vasco da Gama is said to have originally stopped here in 1498 before sailing to Kozhikode in Kerala

Sea walk

The Malpe Sea Walk is built on the edge of Malpe Beach, with the sea on one side and backwaters on the other. It is well kept and great for watching beautiful sunsets. It is around 600 meters long and well built, with a couple of statues embellishing it. It's a nice stroll that ends with some rocks with a view of the ocean and St. Mary's Island in the distance.

Malpe beach

Malpe beach is a virgin beach that is around 850 meters away from the resort. In the state of Karnataka, it serves as both a fishing harbor and a significant port. The exquisite setting for a vacation is created by the boundless stretch of golden sand, gracefully swinging palm trees, and the brilliant blue sky, which set the perfect mood for an idyllic holiday.

Floating bridge

The joy of walking on the waves is available to visitors and beachgoers in Malpe. Along with the rise and fall of the waves, the floating bridge moves smoothly. People can also enjoy the spectacular beauty of the water at Malpe Beach owing to the bridge. The bridge is 100 meters long, 3.5 meters wide, and includes a 15-metre-wide platform at its end so that visitors can take in the natural beauty while floating in the water.
